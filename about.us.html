Little Beginners

Hi, My name is Monique Livingston and I am the owner/operator of Little Beginners

It was always my dream to open my own daycare and in 2022 I was able to make that dream come true.

My love for kids and being apart of watching them grown up was one of the leading factors to me opening a home daycare. We here at Little Beginners believe that providing kids with a home style feeling while being at a daycare helps them transition from only being around family to being around other kids. That transition can be hard on small children and we do our best to help coach them along and encourage them to play with other kids. It is truly amazing to be witness to a child go from being shy and timid to having friends that they look forward to seeing.

We maintain a steady schedule of breakfast, playtime, lunch, music and movement, nap time, snacks, and random games to close out the day. We asisst with crawlling, walking, potty training, and other things that helps your child grow. It is our pleasure to care for your child and provide them with the closest enviroment to home as we possibly can. When holidays roll around we do different arts and crafts activities. We take pictures and do all kinds of fun stuff. When it's your childs birthday we will celebrate with them by having a party with their fellow clasmates.

To all the parents, past, present, and future I want to thank you for allowing me the oppurtunity to be a part of your child(ren) lives.
